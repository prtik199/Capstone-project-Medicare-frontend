Medicare prescription drug coverage Medicare drug coverage helps pay for prescription drugs you need. To get Medicare drug coverage,
you must join a Medicare-approved plan that offers drug coverage
(this includes Medicare drug plans and Medicare Advantage Plans with drug coverage)
Each plan can vary in cost and specific drugs covered, but must give at least a standard level of
coverage set by Medicare. Medicare drug coverage includes generic and brand-name drugs.
Plans can vary the list of prescription drugs they cover (called a formulary)
and how they place drugs into different "tiers" on their formularies.
